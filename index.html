what is the peppership?

The Fresh Ground Peppership is FGP’s new membership platform. Fresh Ground Pepper dedicates its mission to incubating new artistic work and giving resources to the artists creating work in New York City and around the country. By becoming an annual member, you are sustaining a year’s worth of programming that will give artists the chance to engage deeply with their process and lead them to their masterwork.

Over the past 7 years, FGP has jumpstarted the careers of hundreds of artists of all mediums working in the field today. Join our membership and stand in solidarity with our mission to help artists help themselves.

At any level of giving, you will be making a difference for FGP’s artists and their place the landscape of American culture.
